Connected Songs Tutorial

Sometimes you may want to create several songs that are connected to each other in different ways.

For example, a game can have music in different contexts such as main/pause menu, calm gameplay, intense gameplay, combat, boss fight, success and failure. If you want these songs to feel as they were part of a larger musical "package", you might want to create musical connections between these different contexts.

This tutorial explores the different ways that Abundant Music can support such connections between songs.

Ideas

Reusing seed presets for motifs, rythms, instruments, song structure etc.

Just a simple switch between major and minor while increasing/decreasing tempo can be very effective.

Switching to more intense or strong instruments while increasing overall render amount.

Unique levels with different instruments and motifs but same structure overall.
